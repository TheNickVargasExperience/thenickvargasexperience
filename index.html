Who is Nick Vargas?

Some might argue, we ought to be asking: What is nick vargas?
Is he a more highly evolved verson of modern man? Or, is he the manifestation of the concept of perfection?

History

Much mystery surrounds the birth of Nick Vargas. Some say that he was born a fully grown adult. Others say that he simply swam out of the ocean. All that we know for sure is that, much like Jesus, Nick Vargas was born near the end of December.
